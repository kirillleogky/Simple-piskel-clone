Examples

Functionality

You can choose one of three canvas sizes (32x32px, 64x64px or 128x128px) and use 6 tools: pen (has 4 sizes), choose color, eraser, bucket, paint all pixels of the same color, stroke. Also, there is a frame management

All actions available via keyboard shortcuts. In the of the app, you can choose keyboard value for changing keyboard shortcuts

Also, the first drawn canvas is saved into local storage
